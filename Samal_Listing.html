About Samal, Bataan

Samal, Bataan, has a population of 38,302 as of the 2020 census, it provides a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. The town is home to the historic Saint Catherine of Siena Parish Church, established in 1596, which stands as a testament to Samal's rich cultural heritage. Nature enthusiasts can explore nearby attractions such as the Balanga Nature and Wetland Park, offering opportunities for birdwatching and relaxation.

For accommodations, Samal offers a range of options to suit various preferences and budgets. Visitors can choose from resorts like Bluewaters Beach Resort, Kalipay sa Baybay, Kara Kubotel, and Paran Mountain Hill Resort, all providing comfortable stays amidst natural surroundings.

Whether you're seeking a peaceful retreat, a cultural journey, or an adventure in nature, Samal, Bataan, presents a welcoming destination for all travelers.
