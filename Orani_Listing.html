About Orani, Bataan

Orani, a first-class municipality in Bataan. With a population of approximately 70,342 as of the 2020 census , Orani offers a serene escape just 14 kilometers from Balanga and about 110 kilometers northwest of Manila.

For those seeking relaxation, Orani boasts a range of accommodations. Vista Tala Resort & Recreational Park offers villas with private spa tubs and panoramic views , while Sinagtala Farm Resort & Adventure Park provides luxurious villas with private pools, ideal for families and groups . These resorts blend comfort with the natural beauty of Orani's surroundings.

Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or a journey through history, Orani offers a diverse array of experiences for every traveler.
