About Balanga City, Bataan

Balanga City, the capital of Bataan province. Home to over 104,000 residents, it offers a welcoming community atmosphere.

As an educational hub, Balanga hosts esteemed institutions like Bataan Peninsula State University, Tomas del Rosario College, and Saint Joseph’s College of Balanga, catering to a diverse student population.

Tourists can explore historical landmarks such as the Balanga Cathedral and the Bataan World War II Museum, or enjoy nature at the Balanga Wetland and Nature Park, a haven for migratory birds. The city's scenic parks, vibrant markets, and cultural festivals like the Banga Festival offer rich experiences for visitors.

Whether you're seeking educational opportunities, historical insights, or natural beauty, Balanga City provides a dynamic and enriching environment for all.
