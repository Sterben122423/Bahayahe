About Hermosa, Bataan

Hermosa, Bataan, is a serene town where history, nature, and community converge. Situated in the northern part of Bataan, it offers a peaceful retreat just a short drive from Manila.

Visitors can explore the historic St. Peter of Verona Parish Church, a Baroque-style landmark established in 1717. The town is also known for its vibrant local culture, producing garments, salted eggs, and hand-painted jars .

For accommodations, Hermosa offers a range of options, including resorts like Vista Tala Resort & Recreational Park and Steffen Resort, providing comfortable stays amidst natural surroundings .

Whether you're seeking a historical journey, a nature escape, or a peaceful retreat, Hermosa offers a unique and enriching experience.
