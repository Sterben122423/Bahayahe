About Orion, Bataan

Orion, Bataan, is a charming coastal municipality that beautifully blends history, culture, and natural beauty. With a population of approximately 60,771 as of the 2020 census , it offers a peaceful retreat just a short drive from Manila.

Nature enthusiasts will appreciate Orion's scenic coastal areas and proximity to attractions like the Mount Samat National Shrine, which offers panoramic views and a deep dive into the country's wartime history.

For accommodations, Orion provides a range of options to suit various preferences and budgets. From cozy beachfront resorts to budget-friendly inns, visitors can find comfortable stays that complement their travel needs.

Whether you're seeking a cultural journey, a nature escape, or a tranquil seaside experience, Orion offers a unique and enriching destination for all travelers.
