About Morong, Bataan

Morong, Bataan, is a coastal haven where nature, adventure, and conservation converge. Nestled between lush mountains and the West Philippine Sea, it offers a serene escape just a few hours from Manila.

The town is renowned for its commitment to wildlife preservation, highlighted by the Pawikan Conservation Center, where visitors can learn about and witness the protection of endangered sea turtles. For family-friendly fun, Alexa Waterpark provides thrilling slides amidst natural surroundings. Adventure seekers can explore Bataan National Park's diverse trails and waterfalls, while Ocean Adventure offers marine life encounters in an open-sea setting.

Accommodations in Morong cater to various preferences, from beachfront resorts like Playa La Caleta and Camayan Beach Resort to family-friendly options such as Bayandati Resort and Bataan White Corals Beach Resort. For budget-conscious travelers, numerous inns and Airbnb rentals provide comfortable stays.

Whether you're seeking relaxation on pristine beaches, engaging in eco-tourism, or embarking on outdoor adventures, Morong presents a diverse array of experiences for every traveler.
